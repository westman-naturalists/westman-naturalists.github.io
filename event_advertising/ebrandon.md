Saturday, Apr 25 2026 - 9:00 AM
Assiniboine Food Forest

Westman Naturalists - Brandon and Area City Nature Challenge

The City Nature Challenge is an annual event organized with iNaturalist. Communities across the world can create a project to document species observed around their area at the end of April. This is the fifth year that Brandon has taken part! [Join the project](https://www.inaturalist.ca/projects/city-nature-challenge-2026-brandon-area) and submit your own observations around the city from April 24th to the 27th!

We'll be hosting a walk on the second day of the challenge, April 25th, at the Assiniboine Food Forest. Bring your phone with iNaturalist or Seek installed to document observations out on the trail, or take photos and upload them later. The walk will be around 1.5km. Also, it'll be maple syrup season, if you're interested in seeing the operation there!

**Participation**: No sign up is required for this event, though there will be a waiver to sign.

Saturday, Apr 11 2026 - 9:00 AM
North of Virden

Westman Naturalists - Spring Hawk Watch

Many species are making their way north for migration, and this weekend in April should give us a chance to see plenty of hawks riding thermals over the Assiniboine Valley north of Virden! We held our first spring hawk watch last year, so we'll see how the numbers compare a year (plus a week) later!

Please bring a lawn chair if you have one, we will be sitting at one spot for a few hours. Snacks, coffee, etc. are a good idea, along with binoculars, camera, and/or a scope! We expect to be watching hawks until just after lunch or thereabouts.

**Participation**: Please complete this short form (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe80OOHlrU6v0mLDr6ipLKM-RvCcThdvNke_sG4EO7d2898aA/viewform) to sign-up for this event. We will email you with specific details a day or so before the event. Car pooling might be available.
