Saturday, Jun 20 2026 - 9:00 AM
South of Oak Lake

Westman Naturalists - Wilderness on the Farm

We'll be exploring a wild property south of Oak Lake, walking along mowed trails up and down sandhills and through meadows, woodlands, and marshes. In past years we've gone out in later August, so we can expect to find different species in bloom this time! And we'll be on the lookout for Lark Sparrow and other interesting birds.

This outing involves walking along trails that will have Poison Ivy and roots or branches to trip on. Please be prepared, you will need to bring a good pair of walking shoes, sunscreen, bug protection, water, snacks and lunch. The walk is planned to be between 2-3km.

**Participation**: Please complete this [short form](https://forms.gle/z8wSpBjtm11kdjP86) to sign-up for this event. We will email you with specific details a day or so before the event. Car pooling might be available.

Sunday, Jun 28 2026 - 8:00 AM
Lauder Sandhills

Westman Naturalists - Wild Rare Flowers

Join us for our annual trip to an MHC property near Lauder to check out a remarkable natural habitat in western Manitoba. We expect to find the rare Western Spiderwort, hopefully in flower, a variety of other native wildflowers, and Spinystar cacti! There will also be a chance to see Lark Sparrows, Eastern Towhees, and Smooth Green Snakes.

This is an early morning outing, departing from Brandon at 7am. There will be an option to explore the surrounding area following our visit to the sandhills.

There are moderately steep sandhills in the area to climb or you can keep to the flatter, generally sandy, terrain. There will be 2-3km of walking. Please wear appropriate clothing and footwear and bring water and snacks/lunch. Don’t forget insect repellent as the usual ticks and mosquitoes may be joined by sand flies or black flies. There is also Poison Ivy at this site!

**Participation**: Please complete this [short form](https://forms.gle/fRJ39HCDdvyNpk7d6) to sign-up for this event. We will email you with specific details a day or so before the event. Car pooling might be available.

Sunday, Jul 05 2026 - 8:00 AM
Griswold Meadow

Westman Naturalists - Botany and Butterflies

Join us at a saline damp meadow near the Griswold marsh, west of Brandon. Explore a beautiful range of native flowers and grasses, together with butterflies. This is an early morning outing, starting at 8am at the site. There will be an option to check for birds at Griswold marsh following our botanizing.

We will be exploring a wild meadow, it is fairly flat with a few uneven and damp areas. You can choose how far you walk, from 100m to about 1.5km. Please wear appropriate clothing and footwear and bring water and snacks/lunch. A lawn chair may also come in handy. Don’t forget insect repellent as there will be the usual ticks and mosquitoes.

**Participation**: Please complete this [short form](https://forms.gle/4S1z6YqC4iVD1XzF6) to sign-up for this event. We will email you with specific details a day or so before the event. Car pooling might be available.
